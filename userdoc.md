![Main Interface Overview](https://i.imgur.com/y7ktqtP.png)

### Steps to generate a team:
1. **Add Players**: Press the **Add Players** button (marked as #3 in the screenshot above with the **+** sign).
2. **Data Entry**: In the textbox, enter player details as comma-separated values: `NAME, SKILL LEVEL, GENDER`.
   - *Note: Name is required; other values are optional.*
3. **Confirm**: Press the **Add** button to add them to your roster.
4. **Generate**: Press the **Generate Team** button (the icon with the clock) to automatically split the players into balanced teams.

### Change Number of Teams or Generation Logic
By default, the app generates 4 teams with balanced skill levels and gender distribution. You can customize these settings to fit your specific needs:

1. Click the **Settings** button (palette icon) on the bottom right of the main screen.
2. Adjust the "Players per team" or select a different "Balance Strategy" (e.g., Fair Mix, Skill Balance, or Random).

![Settings Screen Preview](https://i.imgur.com/czQJm7Q.png)

### üóìÔ∏è Entering Data from Meetup
If you are organizing a session through Meetup, you can quickly import your attendance list:

![Meetup Import Process](https://i.imgur.com/y2lzEsy.png)

1. **Copy List**: Copy the Meetup attendance list from your desktop or mobile browser. (On iPhone, ensure you load Meetup in the browser rather than the app for easier copying).
2. **Paste & Format**: Open the **Add Players** menu, paste the copied text into the bulk input area, and press **"Format Text From Meetup"**.
3. **Apply Defaults**: Press **"Add default skill level and gender"** to automatically assign a level of 3 and "Male" gender to entries missing this data.
4. **Validate**: Press **"Check/Validate"** to ensure the list looks correct.
5. **Finalize**: Press **"Add All"** to update your player roster.
